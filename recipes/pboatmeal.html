Peanut Butter Banana Oatmeal

This is a picture of Peanut butter oatmeal

Peanut butter oatmeal is a healthy breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Peanut Butter
  • Banana
  • Milk of your choice
  • Crushed nuts
  • Optional sweetener

Steps

  1. Put oats in a jar
  2. Add oatmilk or any other milk
  3. Add banana slices
  4. Add crushed nuts
  5. Put in the fridge overnight
  6. Emjoy
