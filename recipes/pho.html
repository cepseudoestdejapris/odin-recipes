Pho is a vietnamese dish made of tasty ingredients

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • meat
  • spices
  • water
  • lime
  • coriander

Steps

  1. Cook bones in water to make a tasty broth
  2. Put lots of spices
  3. Cook for several hours
  4. Cook the noodles
  5. Pour the broth in a bowl over the noodles and meat
  6. Emjoy
